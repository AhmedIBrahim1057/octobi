The Problem! (Challenges)

  1. Manual processes with large number of students, staff instructors & are exhaustive with time & cost inefficiency. - Manual admissions with lots of applicants. - Management of courses and instructors in course offerings & registrations. - Manual exam control & gradings, longer time to announce results and statistics. - Services, requests & payments.
  2. Lack of control & monitoring of stakeholders. - Quality control of the offered courses. - Students’ questionnaires & feedback. - Required workflow approvals & signatures. - Accreditation requirements.
  3. No Real-time statistics and KPIs leads to inefficient and slow decision making.
  4. Inappropriate documentation of information and documents.
  5. Inconsistent and scattered information between different systems and sources

Our Solution

01

Automated workflows that offer the needed processes by students, staff and instructors, with fast & high availability online platform.

02

Dashboards with Instant real-time statistics, KPIs & reports according to user roles.

03

One reliable solution with all information, all integrated & connected.

04

Different roles & privileges for all stakeholders.

User Access & Management

Module (1)

  • Authentication using official university email address.
  • User Dashboard, tailored for every user category.
  • User Profile Editing. Possible to edit some information, & some information has to be edited by relevant university official.
  • User Listing for different User Categories:
    • Staff
    • Student
    • Employee
  • Definition of User Roles. Specific privileges for every role.
  • Access control on different levels:
    • Faculty
    • Program
    • Department

Student Admission

Module (2)

  • Admission types:
    • Direct from High School.
    • Transfer from another University.
    • Definition of Admission & Transfer Terms, settings regarding fees & minimum faculty requirements.
  • Management of Applicants and review of documents, advance though admission cycle. Account for placement tests and admission tests
    • Students apply online through the university website
    • Possibility to update profile, & online payment of fees
  • Management of Course Equivalence for Transfer students.
  • Distribution of Applicants to faculties and programs.
  • Admission Fees payment management.
  • Convert Applicants into Students.
  • Reports and Statistics.

Study Management

Module (3)

  • Academic Settings (Faculties – Departments – Bylaws – Courses – Programs).
  • Accommodates Credit Hour Systems and Semester Based Systems.
    • Show the Program and Course catalog on the public website.
  • Definition of Academic Semesters, settings regarding key dates.
  • Course Offerings, define the marks distribution among assessment methods. Nominate teachers and examiners. Link course offerings.
  • Manage groups and subgroups (sections).
  • Define the study schedule. Definition of lecture halls and laboratory space. Link with the course contact hours in the academic settings
    • Show every student his own schedule according to his course registration.
    • how the study schedule on the public website.
  • Management of student excuses (course, semester, academic year)
    • Student apply for the excuse and pay applicable fees online.
    • Student can see status of his student card.
